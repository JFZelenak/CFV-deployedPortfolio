Dipl.-Ing. Johannes F. Zelenak
Fullstack Web Developer

I completed my diploma studies in architecture at the TU Wien as a Dipl.-Ing. in 2015. After that, I worked full-time as an employee in an architecture office until April 2023. Since May 2023, I have made a career change into the IT industry, a field that has always interested and fascinated me.

During my studies, in the module "Digital Architecture", I used the programming language Processing and in 2009 I won a design award for this work at TU Wien. As a senior employee at a small architecture office, I was not only responsible for various architectural tasks but also partially involved in system administration, network technology, automation, establishing a digital office structure, website management, and more. During my work at the architecture office, I automated and simplified various work processes for myself and my colleagues using LISP codes in the AutoCAD drafting software. Furthermore, I have also gained experience with Visual Basic in Excel.

I am proficient in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and during my studies, I created and managed my own blog and portfolio website. Currently, I am completing an online Java course. I have obtained the DigComp 2.2 AT certificate, which attests to my digital competence, achieving the highest level of fit4internet Dig-CERT competence level 5. Furthermore, I completed the 5-Day Challenge offered by CodeInstitute within one day and received a positive participation certificate.
